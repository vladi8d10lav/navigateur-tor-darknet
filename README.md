## What is the Tor Browser?

The Tor Browser is a free, open-source tool designed to enhance online privacy and anonymity. It routes your internet traffic through a network of volunteer-operated servers, known as the Onion Router (Tor) network. This multi-layered encryption makes it difficult for websites, ISPs, or governments to track your online activities.

In 2026, Tor remains a cornerstone for secure browsing, especially amid rising concerns over data privacy and surveillance. It's essential for accessing .onion sites, which form the backbone of the dark&#8197;net—a hidden layer of the internet not indexed by standard search engines like Google.
